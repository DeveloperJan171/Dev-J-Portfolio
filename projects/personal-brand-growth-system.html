OVERVIEW

The Challenge

The client had no existing online presence, visual identity, or customer acquisition system. The goal was to create a complete digital foundation capable of attracting, nurturing, and converting potential clients.

BRAND IDENTITY

Building a Consistent Visual Foundation

Before developing the website and marketing system, a complete brand identity was established to ensure consistency across all digital touchpoints.

Three distinct brand directions were explored, each communicating a different emotional tone and positioning strategy before selecting the final direction.

Concept 01

Nature & Guidance

Concept 02

Transformation & Growth

Concept 03

Alignment & Abundance

SELECTED DIRECTION

Developing the Final Brand Identity System

Concept 01 was selected as the final direction and expanded into a comprehensive brand style guide. The system established clear standards for logo usage, typography, color application, imagery, and supporting visual assets to ensure consistency across all marketing materials and digital touchpoints.

CREATIVE HIGHLIGHTS

Key Deliverables Across the Campaign

Rather than creating isolated graphics, every asset was designed as part of a unified marketing ecosystem. Each deliverable served a specific objective—from introducing the brand to educating the audience and encouraging consultation bookings.

01 • BRAND AWARENESS

Introducing the Brand

The campaign began by introducing the client's visual identity, values, and overall mission. These creatives established a consistent first impression while encouraging visitors to engage with future educational content.

02 • EDUCATIONAL CONTENT

Building Authority Through Value

Educational posts transformed complex wellness concepts into easy-to-understand content, helping establish trust while encouraging continued engagement across the campaign.

03 • Authority Building

Strengthening Trust Through Motion

Short-form motion graphics transformed educational concepts into engaging visual experiences, reinforcing the brand's credibility while increasing audience engagement.

04 • CONVERSION

Encouraging Consultation Bookings

The campaign concluded with conversion-focused creatives that guided qualified leads toward downloading the guide and booking a consultation through the marketing funnel.

LEAD MAGNET

Creating a Value-Driven Entry Point

An 11-page lead magnet was developed to provide value before asking for a consultation. The guide introduces the coach's philosophy, offers actionable insights, and encourages readers to take the next step through a consultation booking process.
